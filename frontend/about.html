Project Overview

The Future of Public Safety

Advanced Surveillance Gun Detection bridges the gap between Computer Vision and real-world security. We transform standard CCTV feeds into active threat-neutralization sensors using YOLOv8.

The Crisis

Manual Monitoring is Obsolete

Security guards suffer from "Screen Fatigue" after just 20 minutes of continuous monitoring. In critical moments where weapons are drawn, seconds determine lives.

  • Reactive: Most systems only help after an incident occurs.
  • Fatigue: Human attention span is the weakest link in security.
  • Scaling: Impossible to monitor 100+ cameras simultaneously with humans.
The Solution

AI-Powered Automation

We built a centralized Advanced Surveillance Gun Detection Engine that leverages custom-trained YOLOv8 models. It performs millisecond-level frame analysis to detect tactical threats.

Autonomous Vigilance

The system autonomously classifies threats (Handguns, Knives, Intruders) and pushes instant alerts to relevant authorities before a crime escalates.
